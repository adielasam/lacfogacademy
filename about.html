Principal's Message

A Word of Welcome

Welcome to Lacfog Academy — a school where every child is seen, heard, and equipped to achieve their full potential. Since our founding, we have been passionate about creating an environment where academic excellence and strong character development go hand in hand.

We believe education is not merely about passing examinations; it is about building confident, curious, and compassionate individuals who are ready to lead in a rapidly changing world. Our teachers are dedicated professionals who inspire a genuine love for learning in every classroom.

Whether your child is joining us at Nursery or Senior Secondary level, I invite you to explore everything Lacfog has to offer. We would be honoured to partner with your family on this journey.

The Principal Lacfog Academy, Port Harcourt
Since Our Beginning

Lacfog's Story

A journey of growth, community, and commitment to education in Rivers State.

2020
Est.

The Beginning

Lacfog Academy was founded with a clear vision: to provide accessible, quality education from Nursery through Senior Secondary School to children in the Choba community and beyond.

2021

Growing Our Programmes

We expanded our class offerings and launched structured Primary and Junior Secondary programmes, welcoming our first cohort of dedicated teaching staff and over 80 enrolled students.

2022

STEM & Technology Focus

A dedicated STEM laboratory and ICT suite were commissioned, reflecting our commitment to preparing students for a technology-driven future and aligning with national curriculum goals.

2024

Senior School Launch & First WAEC Results

Our Senior Secondary School welcomed its inaugural class. Our first cohort of WAEC candidates posted outstanding results, affirming the quality of our academic foundation.

Today

Excellence Continues

With over 121 students, 30+ qualified teachers, and a thriving campus culture, Lacfog Academy continues to build the next generation of leaders in Port Harcourt and across Nigeria.

What We Stand For

Ethos & Vision

Our values guide every decision, every lesson, and every relationship at Lacfog Academy.

Our Vision

To be a centre of excellence in education that develops well-rounded, morally upright, and academically outstanding leaders who positively impact society.

Our Mission

To provide a safe, stimulating, and inclusive learning environment where every child is supported, challenged, and celebrated — from their first day in Nursery through Senior Secondary graduation.

Core Values

Integrity, excellence, discipline, and community. We believe character is as important as academic achievement, and we nurture both with equal dedication.

Inclusion & Belonging

Every child at Lacfog belongs. We foster a culture of respect and inclusion where diverse backgrounds and learning styles are embraced as strengths.

Academic Excellence

We hold high expectations for every student, supported by rigorous curriculum delivery, regular assessment, and a staff culture of continuous professional development.

Digital Readiness

Technology is embedded in how we teach and how students learn. We prepare every graduate for a world where digital fluency is an essential life skill.
